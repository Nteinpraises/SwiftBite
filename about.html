About SwiftBite

How a small idea became a global fast food movement

Home › About

We Started Because Good Food Shouldn't Be Expensive

SwiftBite was founded in 2019 in Douala, Cameroon by two friends who were tired of choosing between fast and affordable. The first kitchen was a repurposed garage with two fryers and one delivery bike.

Five years later, we operate in 120+ countries, employ over 4,000 people, and serve millions of orders a month — all while keeping prices where they've always been: fair.

Our Mission

To make fast, quality food genuinely accessible to everyone — regardless of where in the world they live or how much is in their wallet. No compromises on taste. No markup for show.

Our Vision

To be the world's most trusted fast food brand by 2030 — not just by volume, but by the people who depend on us every day. Available in every country, loved in every city.

Our Values

Fresh over frozen. Speed without shortcuts. Honesty in pricing. Respect for the people behind every order — cooks, drivers, customers alike.
